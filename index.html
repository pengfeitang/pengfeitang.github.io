biography

I am currently a PhD student in the Department of Information Systems, School of Economics and Management at Beihang University. I obtained my bachelor's degree in Information Management and Information Systems from Beihang University in 2015. My research interests include adoption of IS/IT, design of online platform IT artifacts, and consumption of digital information goods. I am currently passionate and focused on exploring the impacts of website design and information presentation on users' decision-making behaviors.
