biography

I obtained my PhD in Management Science and Engineering from Beihang University. My research interests include adoption of IS/IT, design of online platform IT artifacts, e-WOMs, and consumption of digital information goods. I am currently passionate and focused on exploring the impacts of website design and information presentation on users' decision-making behaviors.
